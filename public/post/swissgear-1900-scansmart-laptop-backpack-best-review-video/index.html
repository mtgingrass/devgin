The Swissgear 1900 ScanSmart™ Laptop Backpack is built tough and is equipped with an extra large main compartment. The ziippers are rugged and oversized. This makes it simple to use with gloves on.It features some adjustable side compression straps, a padded back panel with plenty of ventilation.

This is the backpack, heavily loaded with probably 50+ pounds of gear.

ScanSmart 1900 Backpack fully loaded with two laptops

The ScanSmart lay-flat technology allows for the laptop-in-case TSA scanning for most portable computers. Actually, I have never used this feature, but it’s there.

I purposely bought this Swiss Gear Backpack so I can hold two laptops at the same time and I am very impressed. In fact, there have been times I held three laptops in this bad boy! Don’t ask!

The backpack is made out of a combination of fabric and polyester and it holds up great! It has enough cushion that I feel safe setting it on the floor without much impact to my gear.

I posted this video a few years ago. So many people commented and said there were going to buy this bag since then. I am not affilaited in any way with this product directly. The images don’t do this backpack justice. You should watch the video if you made it this far.

The ScanSmart 1900 laptop backpack also has two water bottle pockets which I use constantly. Actually, I use one pocket for a water bottle and the other for my tripod (it has a strap to hold it down pretty good).

It has an easy access laptop compartment (side entry) but to be honest, both laptop entrances are easy access, in my opinion.
